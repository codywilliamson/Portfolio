My Story...

Hey there. I’m a self-taught front end web developer. I’ve been teaching myself to code for over five years now, with a specific focus in web development for the last two. I have a serious passion for building something that is functionally beautiful. I always strive for the “it just works” philosophy for my end users.

Although, most of my free time is spent increasing my dev skills, I have other interests as well. Development is my main focus, but I consider at least the basics of design to be an integral part in any web developer's skill set. I also enjoy wrenching on cars, amateur photography, and lovin’ on my dogs.

The most important part of my life is my wife and son. Without them, I wouldn’t be who I am today. I definitely wouldn’t be the developer I am without their motivation for me to succeed.

Anyways, that’s about it. If you’re a potential employer or a prospective client, let’s connect. I look forward to working with you!

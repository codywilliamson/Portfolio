How I Do Things...

Consult

We'll get together and discuss your vision. Static site? Dynamic, add-your-content? We'll decide what's best for you, and move to the next step...

Design

I can design it myself, or if you've already got something in mind, we'll work with that. This is when you will provide me with the copy and images for your site.

Develop

Time to get to work. I'll get to work doing what I love, and get your site ready for deployment.

Revise

Time to check things out. If there are some changes you'd like to see, more things to add*, or to set up plans for hosting, this is the time to ask!

Complete!

... and that's it! Your site is ready to go and handed over to you. Time to enjoy your new website.

*Major site designs, updates, and additions once agreed upon will be written out in contract at time of consultancy. If client requests any major additional changes, charges may occur.
